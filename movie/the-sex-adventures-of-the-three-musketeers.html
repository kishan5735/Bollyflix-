The Sex Adventures of the Three Musketeers (1971)

Overview

Director: Erwin C. Dietrich (as Michael Thomas)

Genre: Erotic, Comedy, Adventure

Runtime: 76 minutes

Language: German

Plot Summary

D'Artagnan travels to Paris to train with the Musketeers, but instead of heroism, he embarks on a series of erotic escapades with barmaids, peasants, and noblewomen. The film parodies the classic tale with bawdy humor and adult content.

Main Cast

  • Peter Graf as d’Artagnan
  • Ingrid Steeger as Yvonne
  • Nadia Pilar, Li Huber, Alfred Codona, Yvonne Sollin, Raphael Britten

Reception

Widely criticized for lacking both plot and erotic appeal, the film was described by critics as “boring and silly.” Nevertheless, it remains a notable entry in 70s sexploitation cinema.
