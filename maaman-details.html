Plot Summary

Inba (Soori) is a devoted uncle whose life revolves around his nephew, Laddu. After marrying a kind doctor named Reka (Aishwarya Lekshmi), Inba finds himself in the middle of a domestic crisis as Laddu becomes emotionally volatile and possessive. Reka struggles to find her place in the family, and the couple must navigate emotional trauma and complex relationships. The movie explores themes of love, sacrifice, parenting, and personal growth.

Soundtrack

The music for Maaman is composed by Hesham Abdul Wahab. The songs are a mix of emotional ballads and rural beats, complementing the tone and atmosphere of the film.

OTT and Theatrical Release

The movie was released theatrically on May 16, 2025. Post its theatrical run, it was released on the ZEE5 OTT platform. Zee Tamil and Zee Thirai own the satellite rights.

Reception

Maaman received mixed reviews. Critics appreciated the performances and emotional undertones, but felt the screenplay lacked depth. Times of India rated it 2.5/5.
